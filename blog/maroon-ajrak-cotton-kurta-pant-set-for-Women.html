What is the difference between an ethnic top and a kurti?

An ethnic top is shorter and more versatile, often paired with jeans or pants for a casual look. A kurti is longer and carries a more traditional structure, usually paired with ethnic bottoms. Ethnic Sukoon offers both styles, but kurtis are often preferred for a more complete ethnic look, while tops provide flexibility for everyday styling.
