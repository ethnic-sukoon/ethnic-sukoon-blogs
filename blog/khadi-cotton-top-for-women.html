What is the difference between an ethnic top and a kurti?

An ethnic top is usually shorter in length and designed for more versatile styling. It can be paired easily with jeans, pants, or skirts and is often used for casual or semi-formal settings. A kurti, on the other hand, is typically longer and carries a more traditional silhouette, usually paired with ethnic bottoms like leggings or palazzos. While both fall under ethnic wear, tops offer more flexibility for daily wear, whereas kurtis are slightly more traditional in appearance.
