Which co-ord set fabric is best for Indian summer?

For Indian summer, breathable fabrics like cotton are considered the most suitable option. Cotton allows air to circulate, absorbs moisture, and helps keep the body cool even during long hours. This makes it ideal for hot and humid conditions. Synthetic fabrics tend to trap heat, which can cause discomfort over time. That is why cotton remains a preferred choice for daily ethnic wear, especially for those who want both comfort and practicality in their clothing.

Is cotton or rayon better for ethnic co-ord sets?

Both cotton and rayon have their benefits, but the better choice depends on usage. Cotton is more breathable and suitable for long hours, making it ideal for daily wear and warm climates. Rayon offers a smoother texture and better drape, which can look more polished for short durations. However, rayon may not feel as comfortable in hot weather. For everyday wear and comfort-focused use, cotton is generally the better option due to its natural feel and ease of maintenance.

How to choose the right ethnic wear for different occasions?

Choosing the right ethnic wear depends on understanding the purpose and setting. For daily wear, lightweight and breathable fabrics like cotton are the most practical choice because they offer comfort throughout the day. For work environments, structured yet simple designs create a balanced and professional look. Festive occasions allow for heavier fabrics and more detailed designs. The key is to prioritize comfort first and then align the outfit with the occasion so that it feels appropriate and easy to carry.
